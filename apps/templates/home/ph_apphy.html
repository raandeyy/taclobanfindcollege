Program Overview

    The Bachelor of Science in Applied Physics is a program ideal for students who would like to pursue the principles and real-life applications of physics. The program is designed to equip students with the knowledge and problem-solving skills needed in order to apply physics to the broad realm of practical problems in engineering, science, and industry. Students will also be able to gain analytical skills in developing technology through the fundamental principles of physics.
