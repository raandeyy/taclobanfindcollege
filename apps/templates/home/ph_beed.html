Bachelor or Science in Elementary Education

DURATION: 4 Years


Program Overview

     The Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED) is a program designed to prepare students to become primary school teachers. The program combines both theory and practice in order to teach students the necessary knowledge and skills a primary school teacher needs. The program aims to produce competent teachers specializing in the pedagogical approach of education.
