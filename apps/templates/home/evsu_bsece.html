Program Overview

    &nsbspThe Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering (BS ECE), also known as Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering, is a program that focuses on the conceptualization, design, and development of electronic, computer and communication products, systems, services and processes. Its curriculum is built around a strong basic core of mathematics, physics, and engineering provides teaching and training activities in the classroom and in the laboratory exercises.

    &nsbsp While BS in Electrical Engineering deals with the general aspect of design and development of electrical equipment, such as power generators and electric motors, BS Electronics and Communication Engineering, on the other hand, focuses on the study of design and development of Electronic equipment such as semiconductors and transmitters to be used in distant communications.
