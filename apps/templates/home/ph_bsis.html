Bachelor or Science in Information Sytems

DURATION: 4 Years


Program Overview

    The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) program is a four-year degree program which focuses on the design and implementation of computer-based information systems and other ICT solutions to address the demands in existing business processes. The program prepares students to become IT professionals with primary competencies in the areas of business process analysis and reengineering, change management, systems integration, systems implementation, system evaluation/quality assurance, and software maintenance. The program also requires a Capstone project. It should be in the form of an information systems development and implementation as a business solution for an industry need.
