AB in Islamic Studies

DURATION: 4 Years


Program Overview

    The AB Islamic Studies minor in Education is a program which aims to develop well-rounded Islamic Educators, technocrats of the Bangsamoro, Shariah Practitioners, Diplomats, Professionals working in Education, Halal world market, and Islamic Finance. Bachelor of Arts in Islamic Studies (AB-AIS) graduates can acquire multiple professional licenses such as Philippine Shariah Bar from the Supreme Court (SC), Licensure Examination for Teachers from Philippine Regulatory Commission (PRC), Civil Service Examination from Civil Service Commission (CSC), and Qualifying Examination for Arabic Language and Islamic Studies from Department of Education (DepEd).
