Program Overview

    The Bachelor of Arts in History is a program that equips students with an understanding of both the Philippine and world history, civilization, economies, governments, and cultural studies. It also teaches students how to analyze social, political, economic and cultural issues by using historical facts and information. The program equips students with training in historical methodology to evaluate national, regional and world events in their proper historical perspective.
