Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems program of AMA Computer University is a ten-trimester program designed to produce world-class IT professionals that is not only nationally accredited but also globally competitive and who will be professionally competent, morally upright, and socially responsible contributors to national development. The program is a study of the design and implementation of solutions that integrate information technology with business processes. It prepares students to be experts on the design and implementation of IS in business industry.
