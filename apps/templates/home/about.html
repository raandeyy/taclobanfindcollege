About us

Find College is a system that would help students create the most accurate decision-making on choosing the best college course in line with their future careers. The importance of this project is that it will provide supporting decision-making within the app that students can use to detect the most appropriate college course to take. The application aims to gather information through tests regarding the student's prior knowledge, aptitude, skills, and competencies. With accurate data collection, the system is uniquely helping students with the course information, including the schools where the offered course is available. The basis for choosing the school is through the analysis response of the students in his demographic profile.
