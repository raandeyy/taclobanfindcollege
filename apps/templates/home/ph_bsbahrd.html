Bachelor or Science in Business Administration major in Human Resource Development

DURATION: 4 Years


Program Overview

      The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Human Resource Development Management is a course designed to develop the competency of students to understand human behavior in the workplace. It provides students with tools to understand and uncover the motivations and aspirations of a person to align the strategic directions of businesses towards the achievement of both the personal goals and corporate objectives.

The program focuses on two specialization tracks which students can choose from -- Business Analytics and Service Management. The leading positions in human resources or talent development departments in top-ranked firms in the country await the graduates of this program.
