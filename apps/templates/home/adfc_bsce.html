Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering is an undergraduate program in Engineering that provides students with the ability to apply the knowledge in mathematics, science, and engineering in order to design, analyze, and interpret data, particularly that of private and public infrastructures like buildings, roads, bridges, and water systems. The program also provides techniques and skills to formulate and solve engineering queries. BS in Civil Engineering can be taken for at least four to five years in college depending on the term system implemented by the university. In addition to completing a degree program, the Civil Engineering Licensure Exam is often tried and advised to become professionals in the field.
